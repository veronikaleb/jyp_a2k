About show

← Main page

A2K (America 2 Korea) — is a 2023 talent show that will tell the story of the first American girl group, who studied under the K-pop system. JYP Entertainment and Republic Records are collaborating on this project. The premiere of the series took place on July 13, 2023.

  • Number of episodes: 22
  • Original release: 13.07.23 - 22.09.2023
  • Original platform: Youtube

Auditions were held for participants aged 12 to 16 in Los Angeles, Chicago, Atlanta, Dallas and New York. 11 participants from the USA and Canada were selected, who entered a boot camp in Los Angeles to decide who would fly to South Korea to JYP's headquarters. The head of this company himself chose the participants and held auditions.

In the boot camp, the participants were evaluated according to 4 criteria: dancing, vocals, stardom and character. At the auditions, the code participant received a pendant in the shape of a cross with 4 rhombuses, where each section is responsible for a certain criterion. If the participant passed a certain stage, J.Y Park gave her a stone of a certain color, which was inserted into a pendant. If the participant did not pass, she did not receive the stone, but had the opportunity to receive it during the final stage, where she had to show her improvements and that she was worthy of it. Each of them had a certain color: yellow - dancing, green - vocals, red - star quality, blue - character. If the contestant gets all the stones on the pendant, she will be able to fly to Korea and become a member of a new group.

The debut preparation procedure started on 17 April 2023, about 6 months after Phase 2, and spanned 3 months to decide the members of the American debut team. The contestants must fill four additional empty slots using stones awarded through three performance-based evaluations and an attitude evaluation. Each contestant also owns a character book that can be filled with stickers received from teammates and trainers to represent their weekly attitude. The qualities and attributes that J.Y. Park is looking for are as follows: honesty, diligence, humility A contestant will be eliminated for ranking last twice throughout the evaluations.
